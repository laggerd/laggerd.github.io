CW: Physical violence; Colorful fractals in motion

  • Controls:

    • P for fullscreen
    • Mouse for look.
    • WASD for movement.
    • Left Click for punch.
    • I to increase mouse sensitivity
    • U to decrease mouse sensitivity

  • Performance Issues?

    • Firefox seems to have significantly worse WebGL performance and occasional issues with fullscreening. Try Edge or Chrome

Safe Travels

Protect a mysterious traveler as they make their way home.

After learning how to use wgpu with Rust, this was my first attempt at making a playable game. Originally a standalone executable, I later adapted it to run on the web through WASM.
